vPaste

Productivity

A modern clipboard manager for macOS. Save, search, and organize your clipboard history. Supports text, images, files, and code snippets.

Key Features

History Management

Keep a searchable history of all your clipboard items. Find what you need with powerful search.

Quick Access

Access your clipboard history from the menu bar. Use keyboard shortcuts for instant access.

Rich Support

Store text, images, files, and code snippets. Organize with tags and folders.

Sync & Backup

Sync your clipboard history across devices. Automatic backup ensures you never lose important content.
